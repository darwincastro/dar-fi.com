## WLC's version used

17.9.3

## Yang Module

Cisco-IOS-XE-wireless-mdns-oper

## Prefix

mdns-oper

## XPath

/mdns-oper:mdns-oper-data/mdns-global-stats

## Example:
### Sending RPC or REST???

```json
http://<IP:PORT>/restconf/proxy/https://<IP:PORT>/restconf/data/Cisco-IOS-XE-wireless-mdns-oper:mdns-oper-data/mdns-global-stats
```
